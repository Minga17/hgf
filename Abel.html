Player Bio

I'm loyal to the Club, play for the badge. For me blood, sweat and tears - those are the only credit score I know! As a founding member and second team captain of Super Stars FC, established in June 2025 in Omaha, Nebraska, I bring passion and dedication to every match. I've played in all 8 games of our inaugural 2025 season, never missing a single match - a testament to my commitment to this club. Currently leading the team in assists, I take pride in creating opportunities for my teammates and helping them shine. My journey from Ethiopia to representing Super Stars FC is driven by love for the game and unwavering commitment to my teammates. On the field, I give everything for the badge, and my playing style reflects the heart and soul I put into football.
