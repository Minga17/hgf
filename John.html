Player Bio

I believe in myself, and have the ability to help the team go to the next level. As team captain and the leading goal scorer for Super Stars FC, I take pride in leading by example both on and off the field. My journey includes college football experience and being recognized as the best player in all high school competitions in 2023, which solidified my passion and commitment to excellence. As a forward and attacking midfielder, I bring versatility, goal-scoring ability, and leadership to every match. I'm a founding member of Super Stars FC, established in June 2025 in Omaha, Nebraska, and I'm dedicated to driving this team forward. My Congolese roots instilled in me a competitive spirit and determination that fuels my game every single day.
