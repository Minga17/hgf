Player Bio

Joshua is a disciplined and vocal goalkeeper known for his shot-stopping ability and leadership from the back. He brings composure and confidence to the defensive line. With years of experience and a dedication to excellence, Joshua has become an integral part of Super Stars FC's success in the UPSL.

Strengths & Playing Style

  • Quick reflexes - Lightning-fast reactions to close-range shots
  • Strong positioning - Always in the right place at the right time
  • Command of the box - Dominates aerial duels and crosses
  • Excellent communication - Organizes the defense with clear instructions
  • Distribution - Accurate throws and kicks to start attacks
  • One-on-one specialist - Exceptional at saving penalty kicks
