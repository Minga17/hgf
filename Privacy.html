2. Information We Collect

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Player and guardian details (for youth players)
  • Registration, participation, and team information
  • Payment or transaction details (if applicable)
  • Photos or videos captured during matches or events
  • Website usage data (IP address, browser type, cookies)
⚙️

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Manage registrations, memberships, and team activities
  • Communicate schedules, updates, and club announcements
  • Organize matches, events, and tournaments
  • Improve our website and services
  • Ensure safety, security, and compliance
🍪

4. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and analytics tools to improve site performance and user experience. These tools collect non-identifiable technical data.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling cookies may affect some features of our website.
🔗

5. Sharing of Information

Super Stars FC does not sell your personal data to any third party.

We may share information only with:

  • Club coaches, staff, and volunteers for operational needs
  • Trusted service providers (website hosting, communications)
  • Authorities when required by law
👶

6. Children's Privacy

We collect personal data from minors only with verified parent or guardian consent. Children's information is used strictly for club-related purposes.

Parents and guardians may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their child's data at any time by contacting us.
🔐

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your personal information.

No system is completely secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your account credentials private.
🗂️

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill club purposes or comply with legal obligations. Historical photos and records may be retained unless removal is specifically requested.

⚖️

9. Your Privacy Rights (U.S. & California)

Under the CCPA/CPRA and applicable U.S. state laws, you may have the right to:

  • Know what personal data we collect
  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of data sharing (we do not sell data)
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your rights
To exercise any of these rights, use our Data Request Form below or contact us directly.
🔄

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to check back periodically.
