Player Bio

Sami "Sammy" Kambale has been a dedicated goalkeeper since 2014, bringing over a decade of experience between the posts. Standing at 6'2" and weighing 156 lbs, Sammy combines his physical presence with exceptional reflexes and positioning. Known for his shot-stopping ability and commanding presence in the box, he is a vital part of Super Stars FC's defensive strength. His remarkable performance of 12 saves in just 3 games earned him the Man of the Match award, showcasing his ability to perform under pressure when it matters most.
