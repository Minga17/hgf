The Announcement

Today marks one of the most significant moments in the short but powerful history of Super Stars FC. Founded just months ago in June 2025, our club has officially confirmed its permanent home, a dedicated stadium base from which we will build everything this club is destined to become.

"Every great club in the world started somewhere. Today, we plant our flag. This is our home, our fortress, and the place where the Super Stars FC story will be written for generations." — Club Director, Super Stars FC

This announcement comes after months of scouting, negotiation, and community engagement, a process that has reflected the values of Super Stars FC at every step: patient, purposeful, and always rooted in the city of Omaha. From the first day of training to today's announcement, the club has been building with intention.

The decision was not taken lightly. The leadership team assessed multiple locations across Omaha before identifying the site that best represents the club's identity, a ground that is accessible, established within the community, and capable of growing alongside the club as we push higher through the UPSL and beyond.

📍 Official Home Ground: South High School Stadium, 4519 S 24th St, Omaha, NE 68107 | the official home venue of Super Stars FC for Season 2026 and beyond.
🏟️

Our Ground | South High School Stadium

South High School Stadium is more than a venue. Situated in the heart of the South neighborhood in southeast Omaha, it is a ground with history, with character, and with the kind of community energy that a club like Super Stars FC was built to be part of.

Super Stars FC has officially announced South High School Stadium as the club’s new home venue for the upcoming 2026 UPSL season. The move marks an important step for the club as it prepares for its first-ever campaign in the United Premier Soccer League.

Last season, the team hosted all seven of its home matches at Benson High Stadium while continuing to grow its presence in the Omaha soccer community. As the club prepares for the challenges of UPSL competition, finding the right long-term home became a top priority.

Following discussions with the South High School administration, the club successfully reached an agreement that will allow Super Stars FC to host its home league matches at South High School Stadium beginning this season. The venue will now serve as the stage for the club’s next chapter.

"This stadium represents a new step for our club. We’re grateful for the opportunity and excited to welcome our supporters to a place we hope will become a true home for Super Stars FC." — Super Stars FC Representative

South High School Stadium features a full-size pitch, lighting for evening matches, player facilities, and space for supporters to gather and celebrate the game. With a matchday capacity of approximately 1,500, the stadium provides an exciting environment as the club begins its first UPSL season.

While the team built many memories at Benson High Stadium last year, the move to South High School Stadium signals growth and a commitment to building a stronger future for the club and its supporters.

South High School Stadium has been equipped and prepared to give Super Stars FC the professional home environment the squad deserves. Here's a full breakdown of what fans and players can expect:

🌿

Premium Pitch

Full-size natural grass surface maintained to UPSL standard. 115 × 75 yards of genuine football turf; fast, true, and built for the beautiful game.

💡

Floodlights

High-powered floodlights enabling evening fixtures and midweek matches, perfect for the atmosphere that Super Stars FC deserves.

🪑

Fan Seating

Capacity of 1,500 spectators with standing and seating areas, designed to create an atmosphere that gets behind the team from the first whistle.

🚿

Locker Rooms

Full changing facilities for both home and away teams, built to the standard our players have earned through their dedication this season.

🍔

Fan Zone

Concessions, merchandise, and community gathering space, a place for Super Stars fans to come together before, during, and after every match.

♿

Accessibility

Accessible entrances, viewing areas, and facilities to ensure that every single supporter can experience Super Stars FC, regardless of ability.

What This Means for the Club

The agreement to host matches at South High School Stadium represents an important step forward for Super Stars FC. While the club previously hosted its home games at Benson High Stadium, the move to South High School Stadium provides a new opportunity as the team prepares for its first season in the UPSL:

Impact Across the Club

⚽ Players
A consistent home venue helps players prepare better for matches and develop stronger routines as the club enters UPSL competition.
🏘️ Community
Hosting matches at South High School Stadium brings the club closer to the local community and creates opportunities for fans, families, and young players to connect with Super Stars FC.
🤝 Partnerships
Having a confirmed match venue strengthens the club's ability to build relationships with partners such as WENDÔK and future sponsors who want to support the club’s development.
📈 UPSL Progress
A confirmed home venue is an important step for clubs competing in the UPSL. It provides stability for match scheduling and helps the organization grow within the league structure.
👥 Supporters
Supporters will now have a consistent location to watch matches, gather as a community, and follow the club throughout the UPSL season.
🌍 Club Identity
Playing at South High School Stadium gives Super Stars FC a stronger presence in Omaha as the club continues to grow and build its identity within the local football scene. A permanent home ground helps define the club's identity and creates a sense of belonging for players, staff, and supporters.
"We didn't just find a stadium. We found a home. And everything we build from this point forward will have deeper roots because of it." — Club Director, Super Stars FC
