Player Bio

I am Yezi. I am a midfielder who can play forward too. I love soccer and I dream to achieve more in the future. As one of the founding members of Super Stars FC, established in June 2025 in Omaha, Nebraska, I am proud to serve as the team's third captain. My versatility on the field allows me to contribute both in midfield and attack, bringing creativity and energy to every match. Soccer is my passion, and I'm committed to helping Super Stars FC grow and succeed as we build something special together.
