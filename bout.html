Our Story

SUPER STARS FC — OMAHA, NE

Founded on June 3, 2025, in Omaha, Nebraska, Super Star FC was created to give driven players a place to grow, compete, and represent their city with pride.

Super Stars FC is more than a team; it's a movement. Built from Omaha's streets, parks, and passion, our club unites players from every background under one badge and one purpose: to play bold, fearless football that represents our city with pride.

We train like pros and compete like we've got everything to prove. Super Stars FC stands for growth, respect, and unity, shaping leaders on and off the pitch. Every match, every session, every touch is about pushing limits and honoring where we come from.

Our vision is to become a leading force in regional football, known for our integrity, our competitive spirit, and our deep connection to the community. At Super Stars FC, we grow together; through mentorship, through the grind, and through a shared love of the game.

Our Mission

Super Stars FC exists to develop complete footballers and complete people, technically skilled, mentally strong, and disciplined.

We create real pathways for young people to grow, lead, and succeed on the field and in life, drawing out the talents and potential of every player we serve.

By combining professional soccer standards with community-centered values, Super Stars FC prepares young athletes to become leaders and role models.

Our Vision

To build a respected and competitive professional soccer club rooted in community, unity, and opportunity, where soccer is a pathway for personal growth and lasting impact.

Super Stars FC serves and uplifts underserved communities by providing accessible, inclusive soccer environments that prioritize development, safety, and character.

Our vision: to win with purpose, grow with unity, and leave a legacy where community and competitive soccer rise together.
