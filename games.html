A Historic Moment for Omaha Soccer

The day has finally come. Super Stars FC is proud to announce the release of our complete 2026 UPSL Spring Season schedule, the first competitive league calendar in the history of this club. From the moment we were founded on June 3, 2025, this moment has been the finish line we were sprinting toward. Now, it is our starting line.

The United Premier Soccer League, one of the fastest-growing soccer organizations in the United States, and widely recognized as the unofficial 4th tier of American soccer, has officially placed Super Stars FC into the NE-SD-IA Conference, a tight knit, competitive division encompassing clubs from Nebraska, South Dakota, and Iowa. The league has confirmed our full slate of home and away fixtures, and the countdown to kick-off has officially begun.

This is not just a schedule. It is a declaration. Super Stars FC belongs on this stage, and the Omaha soccer community deserves a club to call its own in national competition. That club is us.

"This schedule represents everything we have been working toward since day one. Every player, every coach, every supporter who believed in this project, this is for you. Our journey in the UPSL starts now."

— Super Stars FC Club Leadership

⚽ Home Sweet Home

There is something sacred about a home ground. It is where a club's identity is forged, where supporters become a family, and where opponents learn to dread the noise and passion of a united crowd. For Super Stars FC, our home pitch in Omaha is more than a playing surface, it is the heartbeat of this club.

The home opener is more than a match. It is a moment of community. It is the first time the Super Stars will take the field with our supporters behind us in official UPSL competition, and we intend to make it unforgettable. We are calling on every soccer fan in Omaha and the surrounding area to come out, wear the colors, and help us write the first chapter of something that will be told for decades.

Our home schedule features five fixtures throughout the spring, giving supporters multiple opportunities to witness UPSL football live in Omaha. Each home match will bring a distinct atmosphere, from the electric buzz of the opener to the high stakes final home stand as the season reaches its crescendo. The volume of home support is not a detail; it is a competitive advantage. Our players hear every chant, every clap, every roar, and it fuels them.

"We want Omaha to feel the sport the way millions of fans around the world feel it. C loud, proud, and passionate. Come out to our home matches and let's build something real together."

— Super Stars FC, Club Statement

✈ The Away Journey

Every great club earns its reputation not just at home, but on the road. The 2026 UPSL Spring slate takes Super Stars FC on five away trips, beginning with our very first competitive league fixture on Sunday, April 5th, 2026, when we travel to Sioux Falls, South Dakota to face Sioux Falls Thunder FC.

That opening away trip carries enormous symbolic weight. It will be the first time Super Stars FC players pull on the kit in official league competition, walking into an opposing stadium for the first time. History does not ask whether you are ready, it simply arrives. We will be ready.

Our away schedule also brings us to face fellow NE-SD-IA Conference clubs, including Heartland United FC, Bana Mboka FC, and Omaha Street Futbol Club, each a respected side that will push us to grow. These trips will test our squad's character, our tactical flexibility, and our mental strength under pressure. They will also forge the bonds between players that can only come from competing together on unfamiliar ground.

We encourage the traveling Super Stars faithful, if you can make the journey, do it. Away support is the purest form of loyalty, and it matters more than most fans realize. Every voice in an away section changes the energy of a match.

"Away games build a team's soul. You are on foreign soil, and all you have is each other. That bond is what championships are built on."

— Super Stars FC Technical Staff

🌟 The Long Journey Begins

Super Stars FC was founded less than a year ago, on June 3, 2025. In the months since, this club has built a squad, assembled a coaching staff, established an identity, and earned its place in national competition through the UPSL. That progress is remarkable. But we are under no illusion that this is the summit, it is base camp.

The 2026 Spring season is a foundation. Every point earned, every match played, every lesson learned on the pitch is an investment in the long-term vision of this club. We are building a permanent pillar of soccer in Omaha, a place where the community can find belonging, where talented players can develop, and where future generations will look back and say: this is where it all started.

The UPSL's promotion and relegation system means that ambition has a pathway. Clubs that perform rise. We intend to be among those clubs. But right now, the first mission is simple: compete with everything we have, represent Omaha with pride, and give our supporters a season to remember.

"We are not just building a team for this season. We are building a club for this city, for the next ten, twenty, fifty years. The schedule is just the beginning."

— Super Stars FC Founding Statement

🏆 About the UPSL

The United Premier Soccer League is one of the nation's largest and fastest-growing soccer leagues, featuring over 800 teams across 41+ states. Founded in 2011, the UPSL occupies the 4th tier of American soccer and features a promotion/relegation system. Season runs April through July.
