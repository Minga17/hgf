Our Story
From an idea on the streets of Omaha to a full-squad competitive club.
June 3, 2025
Super Stars FC Is Born
Founded in Omaha, Nebraska, with a vision to create competitive soccer rooted in community, culture, and passion. The founding squad assembled from the streets of Omaha.
Summer 2025
First Training Sessions & Kit Launch
The squad came together for the first official training sessions. WENDÔK Sportswear stepped in as kit partner, outfitting the team in official Super Stars FC colors.
Fall 2025
BLOOM Partnership Announced
Super Stars FC secured its first official community partner — BLOOM — sharing values of growth, inclusion, and opportunity for young people through sport.
Season Opener 2026
Competitive Season Kicks Off
The squad took to the pitch for the official season opener, delivering an electrifying performance in front of a packed Omaha crowd. The journey had officially begun.
