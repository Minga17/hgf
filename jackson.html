Player Bio

Jackson Mbuangila, a defender/striker, brings a strong mentality to the defense, being aggressive to win every ball on the field. Started playing at Mitchell High School in Colorado Springs, Colorado, with Coach Adam Palmer, Alex Dowdy, and Josh Browning. Played at the varsity level since sophomore year and then joined the Colorado Pride soccer team the next season. After one season with Colorado Pride, switched to BVB Colorado Academy for one season, where development continued at an elite level. Got signed at Southwestern College in Winfield, Kansas as a defender, but had so much more to give on the offensive side, so then switched to striker. Currently with Super Stars FC, I'm the second player with the most goals for the team, proving my offensive capabilities while maintaining defensive strength.
