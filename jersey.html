The Official Reveal

As of today, Super Stars FC's brand-new Home and Away jerseys are officially public, and they have not touched a single pitch yet. These kits have been designed, produced, and delivered, but every stitch is still fresh. Not one game has been played in them. They were announced recently by the club, and this article is the full story behind what they are, where they came from, and what they mean for the next chapter of Super Stars FC.

"These aren't just new jerseys. They are the kits that Super Stars FC will step into the UPSL wearing for the first time. Every player on this squad knows what that means, and they cannot wait." — Club Director, Super Stars FC

The Season 2025 – 26 kit collection consists of two jerseys designed and produced by SQUAD SUPPLY: a Home kit in Bright Red: bold body with black diagonal streak lines, white sleeves, and "SUPER STARS FC" across the chest, and an Away kit in White with red trim, an Africa map printed on the back, the BLOOM colorful logo, and "OMAHA" on the side. Both carry the Super Stars FC crest and represent a major evolution for the club.

🆕 Brand New | Never Worn: These kits were just officially revealed by the club. They will make their on-pitch debut at the first UPSL Away game in April 2026. Until then, they exist only as an announcement and a promise.
⬅️

The Kit That Came Before

Before these new jerseys, Super Stars FC played all 7 competitive matches of their debut season in their original kit, a simple but unmistakable combination that served the club through every win, draw, and defeat in their first ever campaign.

Original Super Stars FC Kit (Season 2025 | All 7 Games): Red + Black. That was the kit worn through every match of the club's debut UPSL run, from the opening win against Togoless SA on August 16, through to the final recorded result. It is the kit the squad built their early identity in, and it will always hold a special place in the club's history.

The red and black served as the club's identity through a debut season that included 3 wins, 1 draw, 21 goals scored, and some unforgettable moments, like Jackson's hat-trick in the opening match, Minga's 90+1' winner against Omaha Elites, John's eight goals in seven games. All of that history was made in the original kit.

"The original kit took us through our first season. Every goal, every win, every hard moment, that was in red and black. These new kits are for the next chapter. A bigger stage. A new league." — Club Director, Super Stars FC

Now, with the UPSL era beginning, the club steps forward with a new identity, more professional, more detailed, more meaningful. The new kits were built for this exact moment.

📊 Season 2025 in the Original Kit: 7 games played, 3 wins, 1 draw, 3 losses, 21 goals scored, 19 conceded. That record belongs to the red and black. Everything in the UPSL belongs to what comes next.

Design & Color Breakdown

Both kits were designed from scratch to serve as the visual identity of Super Stars FC in the UPSL, a higher level of football, and a higher level of presentation to match. Every element was intentional.

"Red at home. White on the road. We wanted two kits that look completely different from each other but tell the same story when you put them side by side. That's Super Stars FC." — Club Director, Super Stars FC

The home kit is striking from across a stadium. The bright red body is broken up by sharp, bold black diagonal streak lines that run the full length of the jersey, a dynamic pattern that mirrors the attacking energy of a team that scored 21 goals in just 7 matches in their debut season. White sleeves contrast cleanly against the red body. "SUPER STARS FC" is printed across the chest in white, no front sponsor, just the club's name taking centre stage.

The away kit shifts registers entirely, a clean white base with vibrant red on the collar, sleeve cuffs, and bottom hem. Light vertical texture lines run the body. The front is composed and professional. The back is where the kit becomes something deeper: a bold squad number in red, the outline of the African continent beneath it, and the BLOOM full color logo at the base. The word "OMAHA" is printed in red on the side.

🔴

The Africa Map | Identity & Meaning

Of everything on both new kits, the most talked about detail since the club posted the reveal is the outline of the African continent printed on the back of the away jersey. It is the design element that makes this kit unlike anything else in the UPSL.

🌍

A Symbol of Heritage Worn on Every Away Match

Super Stars FC was built by and for a community with deep African roots: players, supporters, and staff who carry that identity with them into every match. The map on the back of the away jersey doesn't whisper that. It says it loudly, visually, and permanently. When this squad runs out in white and red at an away ground in the UPSL for the first time in April, opponents and spectators will see exactly who Super Stars FC are and where they come from.

"We put the Africa map on that jersey because it belongs there. Our roots aren't something we set aside when we play football, they're the reason we play. That kit carries our whole story." — Club Director, Super Stars FC

Beneath the number and the map, the BLOOM full color logo sits at the base of the back, a vibrant, recognizable design that anchors the away kit in Omaha's community. The combination of the Africa map and BLOOM together on the same jersey back makes this one of the most meaningful kits in the UPSL.

📍 "OMAHA" Side Print: Red "OMAHA" text on the side of the away jersey, wherever Super Stars FC travel in the UPSL, they carry the city with them. That's not incidental. That's a declaration.
🏟️

The UPSL Debut | First Away Game, April 2026

These kits were built for a specific purpose: the UPSL. After a debut season in their original red and black kit, Super Stars FC are stepping up to the United Premier Soccer League, a national amateur competition and one of the largest soccer leagues in the United States. This is a significant leap, and these kits are the visual marker of that leap.

⚽

UPSL Debut | Away Game, April 2026

The first time either of these kits is worn in a competitive match will be at Super Stars FC's first ever UPSL Away game in April 2026. The Away kit, white with red trim, Africa map on the back, BLOOM at the base, will be the jersey that writes the first chapter of the club's UPSL history. That moment is months away, and it is already anticipated by every player on the squad.

UPSL Era Begins April 2026

The Home kit will make its debut at the first UPSL home game at South High School Stadium, date 12/04/2026. That will be the moment South High School Stadium sees the new red and black streak jersey for the first time, under the lights, in a competitive UPSL fixture. Both debuts are still to come. Both are already legendary in the making.

📅 First UPSL Away Game | April 2026: The Away kit (white, Africa map, BLOOM) makes its competitive debut. Super Stars FC will step onto a UPSL pitch for the first time wearing jerseys that have never seen a match before. That is the moment everything was designed for.

Super Stars FC's move to the UPSL represents everything the club has been building toward since their founding in June 2025. In under a year, a brand new football club from Omaha has gone from formation to competing in a national league, and doing so in kits that represent who they are at every level: the football, the city, and the heritage.

🏆 What is the UPSL? The United Premier Soccer League is one of the largest amateur soccer leagues in the United States, with clubs competing across regional conferences. Super Stars FC's entry into the UPSL marks their arrival on the national stage.

Kit Journey | From Brief to UPSL Debut

The full story of how these kits went from a creative brief to a club announcement, and what comes next:

Season 2025 — All 7 Games
Original Kit: Red & Black
Super Stars FC compete in their debut season, all 7 games played in the original red and black kit. 3 wins, 1 draw, 21 goals, and a platform to build from. The history made in that kit will never be erased.
Early 2026 — UPSL Confirmation
Club Confirmed for the UPSL
Super Stars FC earn their place in the UPSL: a national stage that demands a professional presentation. The decision is made: a new kit collection is needed for this new era.
Early 2026 — Creative Brief
WENDÔK Brief Is Set
Super Stars FC and WENDÔK Sportswear align on the creative direction: red at home, white away, the Africa map as a central element, BLOOM as away back sponsor, "OMAHA" side print. Two kits that tell the full story of the club.
Early 2026 — Design & Production
Kits Designed, Produced & Delivered
The diagonal black streak pattern and white sleeves are confirmed for the home kit. The Africa map placement, BLOOM logo, and red trim lock in for the away. Both kits are produced and delivered to the club | fresh, never worn.
February 2026 — Official Reveal
Club Posts the Official Announcement
Super Stars FC officially reveal both kits to the public for the first time. Zero games played in them. The reveal is the moment supporters get their first real look at the jerseys that will carry the club into the UPSL.
April 2026 — UPSL Away Game
The Kits See a Pitch for the First Time
Super Stars FC play their first-ever UPSL Away game. The away kit: white, red trim, Africa map, BLOOM, is worn competitively for the first time in the club's history. Everything was designed for this moment.
