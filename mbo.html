Player Bio

Gloire Mbo Mwabila is a Congolese defender born in Kinshasa, Democratic Republic of Congo, where football has been part of his life from an early age. Growing up in a football culture, he developed a strong passion for the game and a competitive mentality that continues to drive his development today. This is his third season competing in the UPSL, after previously playing with Omaha Street FC, where the team reached the National Quarterfinals. Known for his defensive discipline, work ethic, and commitment to the team, Gloire brings consistency, leadership, and determination both on and off the field. He continues to focus on improving every season and contributing to the growth and success of Super Stars FC while pursuing higher opportunities in the game. Since joining Super Stars FC in 2026, Mr Mbo has been a cornerstone of the defense.
