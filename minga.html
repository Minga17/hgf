Player Bio

My name is Emmanuel Mingashanga Ngolo, but mostly friends and family members call me by the name Minga. I'm 23 years old, and I love playing soccer, which is one of my hobbies. I play both midfield and forward. I also use both of my feet - right and left. I am a dedicated and hardworking player known for strong teamwork, discipline, and passion for the game. Always focused on improving my skills and supporting my teammates. Since joining Super Stars FC as a founding member in June 2025, I've played all 8 games of our inaugural season in Omaha, Nebraska. Currently, I'm the second player on the team with the most assists and the third player with the most goals, contributing significantly to our attack.

Strengths & Playing Style
