Player Bio

I'm Rey from DR Congo 🇨🇩. I'm a soccer ⚽️ player who also plays basketball 🏀 for fans. I started my career in Africa, initially playing as a striker (number 9), but then my former coach moved me to center defender. I also play as a central midfielder. I like playing so aggressively - that's my spirit. I don't like losing a ball. I like physical play, and my spirit is higher than my play. I also believe in God for everything 🙏🏽 - the rest we leave it for the future. Since joining Super Stars FC in 2026, I've brought my aggressive defensive style and versatility to the team in Omaha, Nebraska. My journey from striker to defender shows my adaptability and willingness to do whatever the team needs.
