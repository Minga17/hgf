Who We Are

Super Stars FC is more than a football club. We are a community-driven organization focused on player development, discipline, and opportunity. Our team works together to create an environment where athletes can grow on and off the pitch.

From the coaches who invest daily in player growth to the leadership team that keeps the club moving forward, every person at Super Stars FC is aligned with one mission: building the next generation of athletes and leaders through the game we love.

Our Values

📈

Development

We focus on long-term player growth, not just short-term results. Every session is designed to make players better technically, mentally, and physically.

💪

Discipline

Respect, commitment, and accountability guide everything we do. Discipline on and off the pitch is the foundation of every great player and team.

🤝

Community

Football is our tool to build confidence, unity, and opportunity for the people of Omaha. We represent our city with pride in everything we do.

🏆

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest standards — in training, in competition, and in how we treat each other. Excellence is a daily choice.
